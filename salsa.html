HOMEMADE SALSA

Salsa from the Farm is a product label design for my family's homemade salsa. The spicyness scale is written in German, a nod to the farm's roots. The design needed to showcase the timelessness of the mason jar. The die-cuts were a fun way to make the product, homemade salsa made with homegrown tomatoes, take center stage.
